Our   House

Step into the world of Brew & Bite, tucked away on 14 Loughlane Row in the heart of Galway. Where the aroma of slow-brewed coffee meets the delicate touch of handmade pastries.

Led by Chef Élise Marchand, our kitchen is a space of quiet artistry. With a deep love for seasonal ingredients and elegant simplicity, Élise and her team create pastries in small batches, fresh on-site each day. Nothing rushed, nothing wasted, only warmth, precision, and care in every bite.

Upstairs, our cozy lounge offers a calm escape. Whether it’s a golden croissant with your morning brew or a thoughtful tea moment in the afternoon, each visit is an invitation to slow down and savour.

Welcome to Brew & Bite, out home. A place where craft meets comfort.

Élise Marchand, Chef pâtissier
